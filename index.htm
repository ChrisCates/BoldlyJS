How it works:

Download boldly.js and include it:

boldly.js

<script type="text/javascript" src="boldly.js"></script>

Add a boldly supported tag

<b1>Boldly</b1>
<b2>Boldly</b2>
<b3>Boldly</b3>
<b4>Boldly</b4>
<b5>Boldly</b5>

Boldly Boldly Boldly Boldly Boldly

Due to the nature of some Open Type fonts. <b5> may in fact actually make the text-shadow to appear inconsistent, specifically serif fotns. If you can use font-weight: 100-900; instead. It is highly recommended.
